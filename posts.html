PhD Student's Quick Ramen Upgrade

15 min 1 serving
Ingredients
  • 1 packet instant ramen
  • 1 egg
  • 2-3 slices of green onion
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • Optional: nori, mushrooms, or leftover vegetables
Instructions
  1. Boil water and cook ramen according to package directions
  2. In the last 2 minutes, crack an egg into the pot
  3. Add green onions and any additional vegetables
  4. Finish with sesame oil and soy sauce
  5. Enjoy your upgraded study fuel!

Conference Coffee Break Cookies

30 min 24 cookies
Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C)
  2. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt
  5. Fold in chocolate chips
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons onto baking sheet
  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden
